Project Idea

Overview

Augmented reality or AR (for short) technology has seen remarkable growth into today's tech world and it is currently getting expanding its growth. The google definition of augmented reality is “a technology that superimposes a computer-generated image on a user's view of the real world, thus providing a composite view”. My project idea deals with AR and how I believe that AR is the next step forward. AR does have many uses but it could be used in a new way to talk or to watch someone dance/sing. For example if you had booked a concert and couldn't make it live, you could watch an AR version to experience it through your smart device. Another way to use AR would be to have it scan a model/object (literally anything) and upload a 3D model copy of itself, this can be helpful when buying something like furniture and not being so sure if it will fit where you have intended it to be. The targeted audience is for everyone, because this is used in a class, work, social and party environment, the only limitation i could think is people who don't have the technology or people who don't understand how to use it.

Motivation

A great example or AR being used is Pokemon Go where the whole game is an augmented reality mobile game, and back in 2016 the game had more than a 500 millions downloads. As i have stated before that AR is growing technology and it said in some statistics that AR will continue to grow over the past few years. According to MarketsandMarkets back in 2019 the AR market is worth $10.7 billion USD and is expected to grow and reach $72.7 billion USD by 2024, they also note that the CAGR will grow to at a 46.6% from 2019 to 2014. Many companies are heavily invested into the AR market such as Facebook Corporation, Intel Corporation, Qualcomm, Inc., Alphabet, Inc., Comcast Ventures, and Samsung Group. Apple rumored product the Apple glasses may be AR smart glasses. I believe that the world of tech is moving towards augmented reality and it would be interesting to see how it would end up being used in society and how it may change the world.

Description

My project idea is to expand what AR could do at the moment. The product itself might be a camera with a wireless mic and an app will accompany the product to utilise the features. Also note some features may require VR. Most people in today's society are using some type of smart device, and as long as it has a camera and compatible specs it may be able use AR features and that means that they could use features of my products and services. My project idea can do more than one thing as it can expand depending on new ideas that come to mind. At best people currently use AR just to change your face with a filter or have a model to replace their face/body or use AR to see what the measurement of a 3D scanned object is. Also some apps/games on smartphones use/require AR technology to work/play.

The main feature of my services is that it enables the user to do social AR where people can talk to each other using either their real face or an made up avatar at any time and anywhere. Similar to how apple use memoji when using your face camera to talk to other people but full body instead. Avatars are created by the users when used to chat. It would be able to capture not only face movements but body movement as well depending if the user allows it, the reason I added this in is because body language can help improve conversation. I believe the user completely utilises this feature if they own some type of smart glasses. I can also see this being used when students have online classes, where face cameras are required and students are using created avatars. By doing this some people won't be so shy about webcamming and this may make the class more interactable.

To further expand on my product or service Virtual Reality (VR for short) could be used to improve my services. My services can also provide a live concert that has been done completely digitally (digital concert), so instead of watching the concert from a 2D perspective users can watch it through augmented reality making it more 3D like and can make the concert exceptionally more immersive that way, the reason for this idea included was due to covid-19 most people had to watch concerts over the net and people online including myself didn't find it worth the price to watch online so making it an AR and VR experience could make it more enchanting to watch.
